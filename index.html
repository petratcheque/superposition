Superposition
Where two things are true.

I feel relieved the day is over.

Yet I want to live it all over again.

I feel lucky I get this time.

Yet I crave something of my own.

I love your curiosity.

Yet I feel exhausted by the chaos it creates.

I want you to take up space.

Yet I feel anxious when there’s none left for me.

I cherish our long days together.

Yet I count down to bedtime with guilt.

I feel joy in our bond.

Yet I grieve the solitude I used to have.

About this project
A personal archive of emotional superpositions.
Two things that coexist. Both are true.
—
